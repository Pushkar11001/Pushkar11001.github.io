About Me

Hello! My name is Pushkar Pal, a recent Biotechnology graduate from SRM Chennai (CGPA: 9.09) who has successfully transitioned into the world of data, now working as a Decision Scientist Trainee at Mu Sigma in Bangalore. My academic journey was defined by proactive leadership and impactful innovation; I was a founding member of our department's first club, 'Bionauts,' and led the Motion Media department. My passion for sustainable solutions was recognized with the 1st Prize at Project Day 2024 for 'Dhaan Prakalpa,' which created packaging from rice straw to combat pollution. This was followed by a government-granted minor project on developing dyes and packaging from mycelium. I further demonstrated my capabilities by representing my college during the NAAC visit for startup innovation, showcasing my project at IIT Madras's IIT PALS, and winning the Best Presentation Award from the Chennai Research Foundation for my final year project. My practical experience includes a 45-day internship in the Chemical Engineering department at IIT Guwahati, where I honed my analytical skills using Excel, paving the way for my current career crafting data-driven decisions.
